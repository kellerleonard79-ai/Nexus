About SGA

The Student Government Association at Pensacola High School is the official voice of the student body.

Our purpose

Serve as the official voice of the student body in all matters affecting student interests and welfare.

Foster collaboration and positive relations between students, faculty, and administration.

Organize and support school-wide activities and events that enhance school spirit and community engagement.

Advocate for student needs and promote a safe, inclusive, and productive school environment.
